1. Download a firmware image (.ota)

An .ota updates the firmware and the app in one file. Download it here (works on any network), then upload it to the scanner over Wi-Fi.

2. Connect to the scanner

Join the device's Wi-Fi, then open its uploader page.

Open Scanner →

If obd1.local doesn't resolve, use http://192.168.4.1.

3. Upload the .ota

In the scanner app, under “Combined update (.ota)”, pick the file you downloaded and tap Flash firmware + app. The device applies it and reboots — the app background flips color to confirm which build is running.

All images (app-only .bin, combined .ota, and USB factory.bin) are also on the latest release.
